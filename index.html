About me

I was born in January 1998, in Querétaro, Mexico. I spent the majority of my childhood and teen years learning among my friends at the American School of Querétaro . I am currently studying for my physics bachelor's degree at Facultad de Ciencias, part of the UNAM, Mexico's leading university. During my studies I have become interested in computational physics and quantum computing, I intend to develop skills among these areas in the near future.
